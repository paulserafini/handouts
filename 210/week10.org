** Correlation

\begin{gather*}
\bar{X} = \Sigma (X_i) / n \\
df = n - 1 \\
\mathit{SP} = \Sigma[(X_i - \bar{X})(Y_i - \bar{Y})] \\
\mathit{SS_X} = \Sigma[(X_i - \bar{X})^2] \\
\mathit{SS_Y} = \Sigma[(Y_i - \bar{Y})^2] \\
r_{\mathit{XY}} = \mathit{SP} / \sqrt{\mathit{SS_X} \times \mathit{SS_Y}}
\end{gather*}
