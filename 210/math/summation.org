$\Sigma(X_i + 1) = (X_1 + 1) + (X_2 + 1) + ... + (X_n + 1)$ \\
$\Sigma(X_i - Y_i) = (X_1 - Y_1) + (X_2 - Y_2) + ... + (X_n - Y_n)$ \\
$[\Sigma(X_i)]^2 = (X_1 + X_2 + ... + X_n)^2$
