* One sample /t/-tests

** Definitions :ignore:

\begin{gather*}
df = n - 1 \\
s_{\bar{X}} = \dfrac{s}{\sqrt{n}} \\
t_{\textnormal{obs}} = \dfrac{\bar{X} - \mu}{s_{\bar{X}}} \\
\mathit{CI}_{y} = \bar{X} \pm (s_{\bar{X}} \times t_y) \\
t_y = \textnormal{the critical value for } \alpha = (100 - y) / 100
\end{gather*}
